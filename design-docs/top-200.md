# Top 200: ideas, suggestions and problems

This backlog was assembled by applying six code-grounded review lenses (feel and motion, accessibility, rendering and performance, architecture, backend and data, realtime and security) to the Wheel of Shame codebase, then mining and deduping the findings into one ranked pool. Cross-theme duplicates were collapsed to a single entry, items were tiered Critical/High/Medium/Low by impact and effort, and the strongest 200 were kept with a deliberate spread across all six lenses. Read it top-to-bottom within a section (Critical first) or jump by theme; each line carries type, tier, effort, the exact code location, and the reason it matters.

**Tally:** Ideas 47 | Suggestions 26 | Problems 127  -  Critical 21 | High 60 | Medium 89 | Low 30

## Feel and motion

2. **No win sting: thunk() is the climax and silence follows the winner reveal** (problem, High, M) - frontend/src/renderers/three/WheelAudio.ts + ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:574 - after thunk fires nothing plays; a short ascending fanfare on spin complete would close the emotional arc instead of dead air.
3. **No pointer indicator marks the winning top position** (idea, High, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:initScene - the winner is read at the top (PI/2) but nothing in the scene marks it; a fixed metallic pointer removes ambiguity about where to read.
4. **No confetti or particle burst on winner reveal** (idea, High, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:574 + SpinResult.vue - thunk fires and the lower-third slides up but nothing celebrates; a short instanced-mesh burst at the pointer tip would complete the moment.
5. **Wind-up is a setTimeout stub with no actual counter-rotation** (problem, High, S) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:505-590 - wind=-dir*0.1 is set but mainStart=startRot+wind is overwritten by the frame loop, so the wheel never visibly rotates backward before launch.
6. **Camera stays static during the entire spin: no push-in on slow-mo entry** (idea, Medium, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:529 - a subtle camera dolly toward the wheel (z 7.8 to ~6.5) starting at SLOWMO_START=0.82 would heighten tension just before the landing.
7. **Upset slow-mo: extend the slow-mo tail for low-probability winners** (idea, Medium, S) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:playSpin - pass the winner's pre-spin odds and start slow-mo at 0.65 for sub-10% winners, stretching the dramatic tail on upsets.
8. **Near-miss tension: no visual cue when the pointer is one segment from the winner** (idea, Medium, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:550-556 - comparing lastTickSegment to the winner's neighbor during slow-mo enables a camera wobble or bloom spike that telegraphs almost there.
9. **Near-miss hesitation when the resting angle lands near a slice boundary** (idea, Medium, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:474-590 - playSpin eases straight to target; detecting a rest within ~0.2 rad of a boundary and inserting a dwell-then-creep sells almost landing on the wrong name.
10. **Pointer pawl never deflects against pegs on segment cross** (idea, Medium, M) - frontend/src/renderers/ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:551 - playTick fires on each peg cross but pegMeshes never move; a brief angular deflect-and-spring gives the tick a visible physical cause.

18. **thunk is a thin 140 Hz sine that rolls off on phone speakers** (suggestion, Medium, M) - frontend/src/renderers/three/WheelAudio.ts:72-88 - phone drivers roll off below ~200 Hz so the pure 140 Hz fundamental vanishes; a kick-style layer (sine plus noise, or raise to ~220 Hz) lands on mobile.

## Rendering and performance

85. **playSpin lands using uniform slices while build() lays out weighted arcs, stopping on the wrong segment** (problem, Critical, M) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:482-488 vs :312-332 - for unequal weights the resting angle points to a different segment than build rendered, so the visual winner contradicts the server winner.
86. **clearGroup disposes segment geometry and material still held in caches, causing black renders after first rebuild** (problem, Critical, S) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:133-146 + :322-330 - segment meshes lack shared flags, so clearGroup disposes cached geometry and material that getSegmentGeometry/getCachedMat still reference; the next build reuses deleted GPU objects.
87. **Label CanvasTextures leak unbounded; clearGroup never reaches label children of segment meshes** (problem, Critical, M) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:133-146 + :366-410 - labels are children of segment meshes, but clearGroup iterates only wheelGroup direct children, so a CanvasTexture per name leaks GPU memory on every rebuild.
88. **identityColor emits space-padded hsl() that older THREE.Color cannot parse, falling back to teal** (problem, Critical, S) - utils/identity.ts:24 + engine/wheelEngine.ts:114 - THREE.Color rejects the space-padded hsl form in older Three.js, so the entire wheel renders as fallback #4ECDC4 instead of per-name colors.
89. **Labels render upside-down on bottom-half slices; rotation has no hemisphere flip** (problem, High, M) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:369 - rotation follows the slice midpoint with no upright check, so every slice past six o'clock shows inverted text on every wheel.
90. **segmentIdAtRotation uses uniform slices but segments are weighted, firing ticks on the wrong segment** (problem, High, M) - utils/wheel.ts:75-88 + ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:551 - the tick-cross function divides 2pi evenly while the wheel renders proportional angles, so heavy-weight rosters fire audio and highlight cues on the wrong segment.
91. **dispose() leaks EffectComposer, UnrealBloomPass render targets, and PMREM environment cubemap on every unmount** (problem, High, S) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:653-668 + :223-241 - dispose calls renderer.dispose but never composer.dispose or scene.environment.dispose, so each mount/unmount leaks full-size GPU render targets, degrading FPS toward context loss.
92. **No WebGL context-loss recovery handler; canvas goes blank with no recovery on mobile** (problem, High, M) - ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:199-255 - no webglcontextlost/restored listeners are registered, so background tabbing or memory pressure on mobile blanks the wheel permanently with no rebuild path.
93. **Every roster change full-rebuilds the scene; updateInPlace exists but is never called** (problem, High, M) - WheelCanvas.vue:50 + ThreeWheelRenderer.ts:434-461 - a single weight-slider nudge rebuilds ~150 meshes and re-rasterizes N CanvasTextures, triggering every VRAM leak above on each keystroke during preview drag.
94. **Stale closure maps worker angles to wrong participants when roster changes mid round-trip** (problem, High, M) - stores/roster.ts:259-272 - the active snapshot taken before the worker call may be stale when angles return; a roster edit during the round-trip assigns angles to the wrong participants.

## Realtime, collaboration and security

214. **CORS allow_origin(Any) with no allowlist lets any site drive mutating REST and WS calls** (problem, Critical, S) - backend/src/main.rs:16-19 - CorsLayer with Any origin/methods/headers plus no auth means any page a victim visits can spin, reset, or restore their session cross-origin.

218. **No rate-limit on spin or add-participant; a client can drain a session in milliseconds** (problem, Critical, M) - backend/src/routes.rs:24-25 - POST /spin and /participants/batch have no per-session or per-IP throttle; a single client can eliminate all participants or flood the DB in a tight loop.
